ARTIST


DWAYNE , is a worldwide singer-songwriter, rapper, multi-instrumentalist, record producer.


Raised in Vancouver, Canada by a family of musicians, Dwayne began making music at a young age and performed in various musical venues in his hometown throughout his childhood.


He started out his musical career in Korea, Japan in 2010.


He is willing to put his music out to the world with the label called “NO FEAR RECORDS”that he found in 2018, Also working on his global Album That will be released all over the world this year.
